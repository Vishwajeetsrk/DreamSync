# DreamSync Content, Links & Information Registry

## 1. Information Architecture (IA)
The platform is organized by the "Career First" principle. Content is tiered to minimize cognitive load.

| Tier | Content Type | Location |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Tier 1** | Immediate Tools (Resume, ATS, Agent) | Dashboard |
| **Tier 2** | Community & Growth (Meetings, Jobs) | Community Hub |
| **Tier 3** | Personal & Legal (Profile, Privacy, Terms) | Account Settings |
| **Tier 4** | Platform Knowledge (About, Team, Mission) | Footer / Landing |
